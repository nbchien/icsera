Submission

All submissions (abstracts/manuscripts) for ICSERA2024 should be submitted electronically using EasyChair platform:https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=icsera2024 and will be reviewed by experts in the fields and ranked based on the criteria of significance, quality and clarity.

Abstract

We are asking authors to submit abstracts (not more than 250 words) by the submission deadline of March 15th, 2024 24:00 PM (GMT +7). Submitted abstracts must be genuine, concise and factual.

Manuscript

The manuscripts must be prepared to ICSERA2024 guidelines in the conference template, full paper 6 pages and maximum 7 pages which can be downloaded the document through the following link.
