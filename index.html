About the Conference

International Conference of Saving Energy in Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning (ICSERA) started in Jakarta, Indonesia in 2009 and has been followed by Bali (2011), Yeosu (2013), Lombok (2015), Bali (2017), Seoul (2019) and Johor Bahru (2021 - online). ICSERA is a forum for exchanging the latest scientific and technological information in technologies for saving energy focus but not limited to the field of refrigeration and air-conditioning industries.
