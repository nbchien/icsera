ICSERA History

International Conference of Saving Energy in Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning (ICSERA) started in Jakarta, Indonesia in 2009 and has been followed by Bali (2011), Yeosu (2013), Lombok (2015), Bali (2017), Seoul (2019) and Johor Bahru (2021 - online). ICSERA is a forum for exchanging the latest scientific and technological information in technologies for saving energy focus on but not limited to the field of refrigeration and air-conditioning industries.

ICSERA 2024

The 8th International Conference of Saving Energy in Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning is organized by Ho Chi Minh University of Education and Technology (HCMUTE).

Conference Proceedings

The Conference Proceedings containing the extended abstracts presented will be distributed in USB. The USB will include papers accepted for presentation, provided at least one author has pre-registered by the deadline.

Journal Publications

Selected and qualified papers will be recommended for publication in the special issue of International Journal of Air-Conditioning and Refrigeration (SCIE) or Evergreen Journal (SCOPUS indexed) with peer review after conference.
